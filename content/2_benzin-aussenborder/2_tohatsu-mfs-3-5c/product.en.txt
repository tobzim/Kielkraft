Description:

The Tohatsu MFS 3.5 C is the lightest 4-stroke outboard in the Tohatsu range: at just 18 kg you carry it to the water effortlessly and mount it on the transom without strain. The integrated tank makes it independent of external cans – perfect for short trips with a dinghy or inflatable.

As an economical 4-stroke it runs clean and quiet, and the recoil start gets it going reliably. That makes it the ideal choice as a compact main drive for small boats and as a dependable back-up. Choose short shaft (S) for most inflatables, long shaft (L) for higher transoms.
