Description:

The Tohatsu MFS6 D Sail Pro is built specifically for sailing yachts. A high-thrust propeller puts the 6 hp into the water efficiently and keeps you moving safely even in wind and waves. The integrated 12 V charging coil tops up your onboard battery while underway – ideal for long passages.
