Kielkraft specialises in outboard motors by **Tohatsu** (petrol/4-stroke) and **ePropulsion** (electric). Instead of a cluttered general store we offer a curated selection, deep product knowledge and honest buying advice – so you get exactly the motor that fits your boat and your use.

## A brand of Boostboards GmbH & Co. KG

Kielkraft is a brand of **Boostboards GmbH & Co. KG**, based in Hamburg. We combine advice, sales and service around modern boat propulsion – from the quiet electric outboard for tenders, dinghies and lakes to the rugged 4-stroke petrol engine.

## Seller and contact – also after the sale

We are the seller and invoicing party. The heavy freight shipment is handled by experienced logistics partners directly from the warehouse. Important for you: **warranty, withdrawal and service stay with us** – not with the partner. Every unit is official new goods with manufacturer warranty.

## What we stand for

- **Specialisation, not randomness** – two strong brands that we genuinely know.
- **Honest prices** – transparent freight and final prices, no hidden surcharges, best-price guarantee.
- **Real reachability** – phone, WhatsApp and e-mail with a clear response time.
- **Reliability** – official new goods, correct warranty handling, secure payment.
