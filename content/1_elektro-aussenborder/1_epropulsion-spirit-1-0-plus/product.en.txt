The ePropulsion Spirit 1.0 Plus packs a 1 kW electric drive into a housing with a built-in 1276 Wh lithium battery. That makes it the clean choice for tenders, small sailing boats and electric-only waters.

At just 20 kg it is easy to carry and mount, and maintenance is minimal. The built-in lithium battery ships as dangerous goods. Choose short shaft (S) for most tenders and long shaft (L) for higher transoms and sailing boats.
