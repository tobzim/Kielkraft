## Your statutory rights

Independently of any manufacturer warranty, the **statutory warranty of 24 months** from receipt of the goods applies to all units (liability for defects under German law, §§ 434 ff. BGB). These rights are always available to you and are **not limited** by any additional manufacturer guarantee.

## Manufacturer guarantee – additional and voluntary

In addition to your statutory rights, the manufacturers grant a voluntary guarantee under their respective current guarantee terms. The guarantor is the respective manufacturer.

**Tohatsu (petrol / 4-stroke)**
For private recreational use, Tohatsu grants up to **5 years (60 months)** of warranty in Germany under its guarantee terms. Conditions include proper registration, the delivery inspection and on-time servicing by an authorised Tohatsu workshop according to the service plan. Shorter periods apply to commercial use.

**ePropulsion (electric)**
ePropulsion generally grants **24 months** of warranty on motor and battery. For the Spirit range, an **extension to up to 5 years** is possible after free registration with the manufacturer. The respective current ePropulsion guarantee terms apply.

The exact terms, periods, exclusions and the procedure in a warranty case follow from the manufacturers’ guarantee statements, which are enclosed with the goods or published on the manufacturers’ websites.

## How to secure the full warranty period

1. **Register:** register your motor with the manufacturer or importer after purchase. We support you with this and provide the warranty and inspection booklet.
2. **Inspection & servicing:** have the required delivery inspection and the due services carried out on time by an authorised workshop and documented in the warranty booklet.
3. **Keep your records:** keep your invoice, warranty booklet and service records safe.

## Official new goods

All motors are **official, brand-new goods** from authorised supply channels – the basic requirement for the manufacturer warranty. We do not sell grey imports.

Just get in touch – via the (link: kontakt text: contact form), by phone or by e-mail. We sort out the process together and coordinate with the manufacturer or authorised workshop. Warranty, withdrawal and service stay with Kielkraft – not with the logistics partner.
